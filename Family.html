Nana the Youngest

The little girl in the middle? That's me alright. I am the youngest child of 4 siblings, but usually people find it surpiring since I do not act like what people would normally associate with 'the youngest child'.

I find the age gap between my three elder siblings and myself to be considerably wide as my sister above me is 5 years older but despite the gap, I like to believe we have a good relationship with eachother. As my parents both work as a I was a child, I found myself to be needing to be more independent but sometimes I can come off a bit distant or cold to others but that's alright.

Roslan bin Mohd Hamidi (Ayah)

Birthday, Dec 30, 1963

This is my dad! He's in his sweet 50's happily retired but still somehow has his ways of making income and participating in social activites as a counsellor, you can count on him to be a loyal listener.

If I had to name my dad's special talent it would be imitating different Malay dialects. My late grandfater was in the police force so my dad had to move a lot when he was younger, hence why he is able to speak various Malay dialects fluently and easily befriend almost anyone.

H. Fariza binti Othman (Mama)

Birthday, Feb 27, 1967

This lovely little lady is my mother.

She has been working as a teacher for over 20 years, she always mentions how she cannot wait to retire and I can see why. Working as a teacher is difficult, she's constantly working both in and oustside school for her students as well as for some extra income. Her students treasure her a lot, even years after graduating some of her students still visit her on Raya.

She's always there when you need her and I think its amazing how she is able to do it so well, I wish I could do more to make it up to her.

Izzat bin Roslan (Abang)

This is my eldest brother, he works as a bicycle mechanic despite being away a lot he really influenced me a lot in my childhood.

If I had to describe him in one word, it would probably be nomad. He's constantly on the move, and he currently lives in Brisbane, Australia with my nephew and sister-in-law. It's been a couple years since they came back to Malaysia, I hope I can see them soon.
